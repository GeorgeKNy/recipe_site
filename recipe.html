Recipe

Chocolate Cake

This cake recipe is a classic and is easy for anyone to execute!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Dark Chocolate: 200g

Butter: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Flour: 80g

Eggs: 2

Instructions

1

Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour

3

Mix everything until you get a smooth dough

4

Bake in the oven
